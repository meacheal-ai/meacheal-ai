## What I'm building

I'm the second generation at **MEACHEAL** — a 24-year self-producing, self-selling Chinese women's apparel brand my parents founded in Beijing in 2002. Instead of digitizing the brand itself, I'm using 24 years of family supply-chain context to build the verified data infrastructure the entire industry needs in the AI era.

The result is **[MRC Data](https://github.com/meacheal-ai/mrc-data)** — the first MCP server providing structured, independently verified Chinese apparel supply chain intelligence to AI agents.

```
3,000+   verified manufacturers
350+     lab-tested fabrics (AATCC / ISO / GB)
170+     industrial clusters
31       provinces
96/100   Smithery score (top-rated MCP entries)
4        awesome-mcp-servers community lists (incl. punkpeye, 85k+ ⭐)
```

Every record explicitly distinguishes `declared` (what suppliers claim) from `verified` (what was independently measured) — closing the structural gap in every existing B2B platform.

---

## Why this exists

Every major B2B platform AI agents pull factory data from runs on the same architecture: **supplier self-declares → platform lightly reviews → buyer verifies on their own**. AI didn't invent that problem; it systematized the amplification — one unverified listing retrieved 10,000 times a day at conversational speed.

Real verification needs three things AI can't do alone:
- A Mandarin-speaking team that can call factory owners and ask the right questions
- An independent textile lab running AATCC / ISO / GB methods
- A multi-year industrial-cluster relationship graph distinguishing real manufacturers from middlemen

MEACHEAL has accumulated all three over 24 years on the buyer side. We're translating that into structured data for the AI era.
